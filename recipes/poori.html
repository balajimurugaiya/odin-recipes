Poori

Description

Poori is a fluffy bread, that is a quite popular dish in Southern india. It is found majorly in states such as TN and Kerala.

It tastes a bit sweet and goes really well with hot gravies and thick curries!!

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Water

Steps to follow

  1. Mix the ingredients and make it into a goey goo
  2. Add lots of oil and let it rest for 1 hour
  3. Fry it
