Parotta

Description

Parotta is a flaky layered bread, that is a quite popular dish in Southern india. It is found majorly in states such as TN and Kerala.

It tastes a bit sweet and goes really well with hot gravies, watery salnas and even thick curries!!

Ingredients

  • Maida flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps to follow

  1. Mix the ingredients and make it into a goey goo
  2. Add lots of oil and let it rest for 4 hoours
  3. Heat it
